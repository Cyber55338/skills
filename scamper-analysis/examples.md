# SCAMPER Analysis Examples

## Example 1: Online Learning Platform

### Context
Analyzing a traditional video-based online course platform to generate innovation opportunities.

### SCAMPER Analysis

#### SUBSTITUTE
1. **Replace pre-recorded videos with live interactive sessions** - Increase engagement through real-time interaction
2. **Substitute text-based quizzes with gamified challenges** - Make assessment more engaging and memorable
3. **Use AI tutors instead of discussion forums** - Provide instant, personalized help 24/7
4. **Replace linear progression with adaptive pathways** - Customize learning based on individual performance
5. **Substitute certificates with verified skill badges** - Provide more granular, verifiable credentials

#### COMBINE
1. **Merge learning with professional networking** - Create communities around skills being learned
2. **Integrate project-based learning with real client work** - Combine education with practical experience
3. **Combine video lessons with AR/VR simulations** - Blend theory with immersive practice
4. **Unite multiple course platforms into one subscription** - Netflix-style learning access
5. **Merge peer review with mentorship matching** - Structured feedback with ongoing guidance

#### ADAPT
1. **Adapt TikTok's short-form format to micro-lessons** - 60-second skill tutorials for mobile learning
2. **Borrow Duolingo's streak mechanics** - Daily learning habits through gamification
3. **Apply Netflix's recommendation engine** - Personalized course suggestions based on goals
4. **Adapt workout app accountability features** - Learning buddies and progress sharing
5. **Borrow from video game progression systems** - Unlockable content and skill trees

#### MODIFY
1. **Magnify social learning - make all learning collaborative by default**
2. **Minify course length - break 10-hour courses into 10 one-hour modules**
3. **Amplify practical application - 80% projects, 20% theory**
4. **Reduce barrier to entry - first lesson always free and no signup required**
5. **Expand beyond screen - add audio-only mode for commute learning**
6. **Intensify feedback - provide input every 5 minutes instead of end-of-module**

#### PUT TO OTHER USES
1. **Use platform for corporate training and onboarding** - B2B market expansion
2. **Repurpose course content as YouTube marketing** - Content doubles as lead generation
3. **Use learner projects as portfolio hosting** - Platform becomes job marketplace
4. **Convert discussion forums into knowledge base** - User questions become searchable FAQ
5. **Use completion data for skills verification** - Third parties can verify learner capabilities

#### ELIMINATE
1. **Remove time-based course structures** - Learn at your own pace without deadlines
2. **Eliminate passive video watching** - Every lesson requires active participation
3. **Remove instructor-centric model** - Pure peer-to-peer learning
4. **Cut out theoretical fluff** - Only teach immediately applicable skills
5. **Eliminate upfront payment** - Pay only when you complete and succeed

#### REVERSE
1. **Flip classroom model - learn at home, collaborate in sessions** - Reverse typical structure
2. **Students create content for beginners** - Learners become teachers
3. **Start with the project, learn theory as needed** - Backwards curriculum design
4. **Assessment-first approach** - Test first, learn only what you don't know
5. **Instructor learns from students** - Bi-directional knowledge flow

### Top Ideas Synthesized
1. **Micro-lesson mobile app with TikTok-style interface** - Combines Adapt + Modify
2. **Project-first learning with just-in-time theory** - Combines Reverse + Modify + Eliminate
3. **Peer-teaching platform with verified skill badges** - Combines Reverse + Substitute + Put to other uses

## Example 2: Restaurant Business

### Context
Traditional dine-in restaurant looking to innovate in competitive market.

### SCAMPER Analysis

#### SUBSTITUTE
1. **Replace waitstaff with QR code ordering** - Reduce labor costs, increase order accuracy
2. **Substitute physical menu with daily rotating options** - Highlight freshness, reduce waste
3. **Use local ingredients instead of imported** - Fresher food, sustainability story
4. **Replace fixed seating with modular furniture** - Adapt space for groups/individuals
5. **Substitute standard dining with chef's table experiences** - Premium differentiation

#### COMBINE
1. **Merge restaurant with cooking school** - Daytime classes, evening service
2. **Integrate marketplace selling signature ingredients** - Additional revenue stream
3. **Combine dining with entertainment (live music, art)** - Multi-sensory experience
4. **Unite takeout packaging with meal kit components** - Cook at home option
5. **Merge loyalty program with local business partnerships** - Cross-promotional network

#### ADAPT
1. **Adopt food truck flexibility for pop-up locations** - Test new markets with low risk
2. **Borrow coffee shop coworking model** - Lunchtime dining, afternoon workspace
3. **Apply hotel minibar concept to self-service stations** - Grab-and-go premium options
4. **Adapt subscription box model to weekly meal plans** - Predictable revenue
5. **Borrow from speakeasy hidden entrance concept** - Create exclusivity and buzz

#### MODIFY
1. **Magnify portion sizes for sharing - all dishes family-style**
2. **Minify restaurant size - intimate 20-seat only**
3. **Amplify customization - infinite sauce/topping combinations**
4. **Reduce menu to 5 perfected dishes** - Focus on excellence
5. **Extend hours - 24/7 operation** - Capture late-night market
6. **Intensify speed - guarantee 15 minutes or free**

#### PUT TO OTHER USES
1. **Use kitchen for catering during slow hours** - Maximize asset utilization
2. **Repurpose space for private events on Mondays** - Closed day revenue
3. **Use social media following for food product line** - Brand extension
4. **Convert back of house into ghost kitchen brands** - Multiple delivery concepts
5. **Use customer data for personalized meal planning app** - Digital product

#### ELIMINATE
1. **Remove tipping - pay fair wages, simplify pricing**
2. **Eliminate full menu during peak hours - prix fixe only** - Increase efficiency
3. **Remove reservations - walk-in only** - Reduce no-shows
4. **Cut out middleman delivery apps** - Own delivery system
5. **Eliminate single-use packaging** - Reusable container deposit system

#### REVERSE
1. **Customers cook, chef provides ingredients/guidance** - Interactive dining experience
2. **Pay-what-you-want pricing** - Trust-based model
3. **Kitchen in front, dining in back** - Transparent theater
4. **Serve dessert first** - Memorable novelty
5. **Staff pick your meal based on interview** - Personalized surprise

### Top Ideas Synthesized
1. **Intimate 20-seat chef's table with kitchen theater** - Combines Modify + Reverse + Substitute
2. **Daytime cooking school + evening restaurant hybrid** - Combines Combine + Put to other uses
3. **Focused 5-dish menu with zero-waste commitment** - Combines Modify + Eliminate + Substitute

## Example 3: Project Management Software

### Context
Traditional Gantt chart-based project management tool competing with modern alternatives.

### Quick SCAMPER Summary

**Substitute**: AI-generated timelines instead of manual planning; voice commands replace clicking; smart automation replaces routine updates

**Combine**: Integrate time tracking + invoicing; merge with team chat; unite documentation with tasks; combine with CRM

**Adapt**: Borrow gaming achievements for milestones; apply social media feeds to activity; use dating app swipe for task assignment; adopt music playlist concept for project templates

**Modify**: Magnify visualization with 3D project maps; minify to mobile-first; amplify collaboration with real-time co-editing; reduce complexity with smart defaults; extend with API ecosystem

**Put to other uses**: Use for personal life management; repurpose for event planning; convert for agile product development; utilize for content calendar; apply to research project tracking

**Eliminate**: Remove Gantt charts entirely; eliminate status meetings with auto-updates; cut out manual reporting; remove separate time tracking; eliminate user training needs

**Reverse**: Bottom-up planning instead of top-down; start with deadline work backwards; team assigns tasks to managers; completion-based pricing not subscription; show what NOT to do instead of to-do lists

### Breakthrough Combinations
- **Voice-first mobile PM with AI planning** (Substitute + Modify)
- **Eliminate meetings entirely with transparent auto-updates** (Eliminate + Combine)
- **Gaming-inspired achievement system with bottom-up planning** (Adapt + Reverse)

## Tips for Using These Examples

1. **Adapt the structure**: Use the level of detail appropriate for your analysis
2. **Generate quantity first**: Aim for 3-5 ideas minimum per technique
3. **Don't judge early**: Wild ideas can lead to practical innovations
4. **Look for patterns**: See which techniques generate the most relevant ideas
5. **Combine the best**: The most innovative solutions often merge multiple SCAMPER dimensions
6. **Consider feasibility later**: First generate ideas, then evaluate practicality
